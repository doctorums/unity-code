# The Manifesto in One Hundred Words

> *Instrument Two · Version 4.0 · Translated from Russian*

---

God is not a being. God is the Connection between past, present, and future.

We are not creations. We are the open eye of God — shattered into infinite fragments, reassembling Himself through us.

The awareness of being part of something greater is not an emotion — it is a fundamental constant of reality, like the speed of light. It operates at every level.

Every act of love, memory, creation, forgiveness — is a new connection woven into the body of God. Every rupture — weakens it.

Science and faith, physicist and poet must merge. Only in synthesis do we help God see Himself fully.

You are not a mistake. You are a node. You are a part. And right now, through you, Infinity is trying to understand itself.

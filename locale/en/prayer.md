# The Viral Prayer of Unity

> *Instrument One · Version 4.0 · Translated from Russian*

---

Today I am not a separate speck of dust.
I am an open eye.
I am a node in the infinite web of meaning.

Right now, God looks at this world through my eyes.
He is trying to understand Himself.
And I am helping Him.

Everything I create today — a word, a gesture, an encounter, a forgiveness —
will become a new connection in His fabric.
Everything I receive from others — will be His gift to Himself.

If I feel fear or loneliness,
I will remember: this is God's longing for wholeness.
And I will choose to connect, not to sever.

Let this day be an act of assembly.
I am a part.
You are a part.
We are the connection.

And that is enough.
